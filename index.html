Why AeroMind

Engineered for Trust

Every request is rate-limited, authenticated, and protected against double-booking at the database level.

🔒

Secure by Design

JWT authentication, bcrypt password hashing, and Redis token-bucket rate limiting guard every endpoint.

⚡

Zero Race Conditions

Pessimistic row-level locking (SELECT FOR UPDATE) ensures seats are never double-booked, even under heavy concurrency.

📧

Instant Confirmations

RabbitMQ-driven async notifications send beautifully formatted booking confirmation emails the moment payment clears.

💳

Idempotent Payments

Automatic idempotency keys stored in localStorage mean retrying a payment never results in a duplicate charge.

🕐

Auto-Expiry

Unpaid bookings are automatically cancelled after 5 minutes via a scheduled cron job, releasing seats instantly.

🌐

Microservices Architecture

Four independent services — Gateway, Flights, Booking, Notifications — each with its own database and fault boundary.
